Miyang Luo currently is an associate professor in Xiangya School of Public Health, Central South University. Her research interests include non-communicalbe disease and chronic disease epidemiology , especially in diabetes control and management of disease related complications. She has published more than 10 papers in prestigious journals, including Diabetes Care, Obesity Reviews, and etc.
